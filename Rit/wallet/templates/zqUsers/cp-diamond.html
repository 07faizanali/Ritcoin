Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy cryptocurrency on Zaan Queril?
To buy crypto on StormGain, all you need to do is create an account, search for the 'Buy crypto with credit card' button in the app or web platform and then you use your debit/credit card to buy crypto and add funds to your account.
How to use crypto trading signals?
To use a signal, simply click or tap the cryptocurrency you want. A trade window will then open.
After that, click or tap the corresponding signal button. In the example, this is a "Buy signal".
If you want to use the signal to open a trade or make modifications to its default parameters, click "Use signal". A dialogue window will open.
You can change your investment amount or leverage by clicking or tapping the corresponding tab in StormGain.
Your Take Profit and Stop Loss levels will automatically recalculate based on the input trade parameters. After that, click or tap "Confirm Purchase (Sale) at price of…"
How to manage risks using crypto trading signals
The probability required to reach a profitable signal is about 65%-75%, depending on the cryptocurrency market situation and the specific cryptocurrency. The signals are generated through technical analysis. So, if you want to take profit, please choose the signals that display a higher probability.
Where can I find the deposit tag?
To find your deposit tag, please complete these steps:
1 - Click 'Deposit'.
2 - Select your Zaan Queril wallet for XRP.
3 - Select Ripple as your payment method.
4 - The deposit tag option will appear at the bottom.
Do I have to buy a whole crypto coin??
No, there's no need to buy a whole coin. Start with as little as you want. Like most fiat currencies that can be subdivided into small amounts (like dollars and euros into cents, pesos and reais into centavos and etc.), cryptocurrencies can also be divided into smaller units. For example, Bitcoin is divided into Satoshis, which are only 1/100,000,000 of 1 BTC.
How long will it take to mine 1 Bitcoin??
It takes exactly 4 hours to get a single mining reward. After you've mined the equivalent of at least 10 USDT in Bitcoin, click the Withdraw button. We'll credit the profit as a trading bonus to your account in 72 hours.
Can I leverage trade with Crypto Options?
Leverage trading is available on Zaan Queril for Crypto Options. Although it is important to note that Crypto Options are extremely volatile by themselves, even compared with traditional Crypto assets. Therefore, we advise that you be extremely cautious using leverage while trading Crypto Options.
