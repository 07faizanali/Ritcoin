How often can I participate in surveys?

You can participate in surveys every 7 days. Each survey you complete will earn you a profit.

What types of income can I earn through Rimberio?

There are three types of income you can earn: Direct Bonus, Level Bonus, and Ads Bonus. The Direct Bonus is unlimited and can be earned every time you activate a package. The Level Bonus is distributed across 10 levels, and the Ads Bonus is earned by completing weekly surveys.

How does the Affiliate Bonus work??

The Affiliate Bonus allows you to earn unlimited bonuses every time you or someone in your network regsiters in Rimberio and take surveys. There is no limit to how many times you can earn this bonus .

Can you explain the Ads Bonus?

The Ads Bonus is earned by completing a survey every 7 days after activation. Each survey completed will earn you $5.5, and you can earn from a maximum of 20 surveys per activation
